01 · The mark

Two interlocking slanted Z's — the rare double-z in enzzero, angled like motion. Reads as speed + automation: work moving through a system and shipping. Primary form is the lime→cyan gradient.

08 · Lockups & clear space

Four approved lockups. Keep clear space of at least one Z-bar height on every side; never crowd, recolour, rotate, or rearrange the parts. Minimum icon size 16px; minimum horizontal lockup width 96px.
